Which code to use for what? A nomenclature guide

- all the files with "_thor" at the end, are connected to the Thorlabs Kinesis Long Travel Stage
- all other files are connected to the PI Mikro Move Translation stage
- no matter if Thor or PI, the structure is the same:
    - main.py: programm to run and main body of the software
    - camera_handler.py is called by main.py and controls all information regarding the camera
    - diode_handler.py is called by side.py and controls all the information regarding the photodiode
    - stage_controller.py is called by main.py and controls all information regarding the stage
    - homodyne.py is basically main.py but for the quadrature homodyne interferometer (this means: 2 photodiodes!)
    - side.py is basically main.py but detection with a photodiode and not a camera
    - side_reference.py is side.py but with 2 photodiodes, where 1 serves as a reference to normalize the signal
